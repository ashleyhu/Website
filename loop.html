Reinventing Cross-Campus Rideshare

Project Info

Product: Rideshare App

Role: UI/UX Designer

Team: 1 Engineer

Tools: Sketch, Android Studio

Timeline: October â€“ December 2018

Over one of my coop terms, a friend and I worked on a rideshare app targeted towards students. As university students ourselves, we found it very inconvenient that most schools set up rideshare through Facebook, a platform that's unadapted for such a purpose.

Problems
1. Facebook lacks features important to a rideshare app, like filtering.
2. Universities are isolated to their own rideshare community, although it's common for students to want to travel between schools.

Our goals
1. Keep it cheap (students are poor)!
2. An intuitive experience for students.
3. A easy switch from Facebook's rideshare to Loop.
