A Little Fun.

I am the kind of person with “10 too many hobbies”.
I always love to have a little fun, but here are 3 of my favourites!

GuZheng

GuZheng is an ancient instrument with 21 strings that was performed to the emperors of China. The instrument produces an unbelievably elegant sound, and I wanted to play it the second I heard it. I’ve been playing it since grade 4, and now I’m at level 10.

Bouldering

Bouldering has become my go-to activity when I am stressed. I love the energy that pulsates through the bouldering gym, and I love the feeling of being able to accomplish something even when the day turns out a little differently.

Woodshop

It’s always a little difficult to get my hands on a woodshop room. But when I do, I can spend hours to days there creating what I call my “masterpieces”. The picture above shows a mini wardrobe I created to hold my jewelry.
